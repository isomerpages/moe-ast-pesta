<p>Aw Kai Yueh believes that meaningful professional growth is rooted in
strong relationships, purposeful reflection, and practical relevance. As
a Master Teacher, she partners with educators to deepen pedagogical thinking
and cultivate communities where teachers feel empowered to inquire, experiment,
and lead.</p>
<p>With over 20 years of experience in education, Kai Yueh has held key leadership
roles including Subject Head (PE &amp; CCA), Senior Teacher (PE), Lead
Teacher (PE), and Senior Academy Officer at the Physical Education and
Sports Teacher Academy (PESTA), prior to her appointment as a Master Teacher
(PE). She holds a Master of Education in Curriculum and Teaching from the
National Institute of Education, Singapore.</p>
<p>Kai Yueh focuses on elevating instructional practice through inquiry-led
professional learning. She designs and facilitates professional growth
across school, network, and system levels, providing tiered support that
responds to teachersâ€™ diverse contexts and needs. Through individual coaching
and cross-school Communities of Practice, she champions collaborative problem-solving
and professional agency. For Kai Yueh, the greatest fulfillment comes from
seeing mentors and teachers grow into confident leaders who contribute
meaningfully to the profession.</p>
<p>Ultimately, her work is guided by a commitment to the joy of learning.
By supporting teachers to design adaptive, inquiry-driven learning experiences,
she seeks to build collective capacity and empower educators to lead transformative
change through future-ready Physical Education.</p>

<h3>Teacher Leaders (TL) Symposium 2026</h3>
<p><strong>Connecting Practice. Rejuvenating the Joy of Teaching</strong>
</p>
<p>PESTA is proud to present the 2026 Teacher Leaders (TL) Symposium — a
professional learning platform dedicated to deepening connections across
practice, theory and purpose. Building on the success of our inaugural
TL Symposium in 2024, this biennial event invites PE Teacher Leaders to
share their classroom journeys with the broader fraternity, unearthing
the thinking that drives impactful practice.</p>
<p>Under the theme “Connecting Practice. Rejuvenating the Joy of Teaching,”
the symposium foregrounds the bridge between what teachers do and why they
do it. Beyond celebrating successful strategies, participants are encouraged
to articulate the educational theories and evidence-informed logic that
supported their decisions — especially through challenging moments. Through
honest reflections and structured insights, presenters illuminate how clarity
of purpose inspires both student learning and professional joy.</p>
<p>Scheduled for Tuesday, 5 May 2026, this in-person symposium provides participants
the opportunities to connect with peers, reflect on their own work and
leave with actionable insights to reinvigorate their teaching practice.</p>
<p>More details on session highlights, programme and registration information
will be available by 30 March 2026. Stay tuned to the PESTA website!</p>

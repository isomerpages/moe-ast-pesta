<h4><strong>2025</strong></h4>
<p><strong>August:</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>PESTA and CHIJ Katong School received the MOE Innergy Award (Commendation)

to help students reduce their fear of heights and build confidence for
Challenge Rope Courses using VR headsets, combined with effective adult
and peer support.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><strong>September:</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>

through the power of narrative.</p>
<p>October 2025:</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>PESTA launched its Team Talk podcast series, featuring conversations with

lessons in character development, team building, and sporting culture for
schools.</p>

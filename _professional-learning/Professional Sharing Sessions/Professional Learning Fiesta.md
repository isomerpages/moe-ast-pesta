<h3>Professional Learning Fiesta</h3>
<p>Professional Learning Fiesta (PLF) seeks to affirm and celebrate the spirit
of professional learning amongst PE teachers. Held biennially, PLF provides
a platform for teachers to share knowledge and connect with colleagues.</p>
<p>Building on the success of the inaugural PLF in 2024, which featured 92
presentations from PE colleagues and partners, and well attended by more
than 1000 participants, PLF2026 promises to yet another …. we anticipate
another engaging and collaborative gathering in September 2026. The upcoming
PLF will continue to highlight evolving practices in Physical Education
whilst strengthening our community's spirit of sharing, learning, and growing
together to create positive impact in PE classrooms across Singapore.&nbsp;
You can expect the following highlights:</p>
<p>Pre-Fiesta Events:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>PEKO Marketplace - 2 September 2026, Wednesday (PM) - Online</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>PEKO Sharing - 16 September 2026, Wednesday (PM) - Face-to-face</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Fiesta Event:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>PLF - 21-25 September 2026, Monday-Friday (PM) - via Google Meet</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Stay tuned for details on abstract submissions, sessions, and registration
— and start considering how you might contribute. Let's make PLF2026 another
meaningful milestone together!</p>
